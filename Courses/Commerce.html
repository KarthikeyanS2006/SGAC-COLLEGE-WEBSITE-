About our Department

Department of commerce was started in the very first year of inception of GACW. The department started with the name of B.C.S (Bachelor of corporate secretaryship). Later it was changed into B.com (C.S).

The prime motto of the department is to equip the girl students in and around the district for self-sustainable. The department is equipped with good faculty members having real interest towards the progress of the students. We are continuously producing university ranks.

Due to the high demand for the course we have two shifts functioning successfully.
