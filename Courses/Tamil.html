About our Department

The Department of Tamil at Government Arts College for Women is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Tamil language and literature. We offer comprehensive programs that explore classical and modern Tamil literature, linguistics, and cultural studies.

Our department is equipped with experienced faculty members who are passionate about Tamil studies and committed to nurturing the next generation of Tamil scholars and language experts.

Vision:

To be a center of excellence in Tamil language, literature, and cultural studies.

Mission:

To provide quality education in Tamil and preserve the rich linguistic and cultural heritage.
